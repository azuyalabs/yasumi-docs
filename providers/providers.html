    Holiday Providers

    'Providers' is nothing but a fancy word in Yasumi for a country or a subdivision: however these are the components of the library that provide and calculate the holidays. Yasumi's focus is primarily only official holidays and non-working days, however other type of holidays or events will be added when possible.
